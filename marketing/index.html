Our Story

String Instrument Sales, Rentals, and Repair.

Mi Vida Strings was founded in 2010 by Denver natives, Eric and Michele Trujillo. The shop is a destination for fine quality orchestral string instrument sales, rentals and repairs. Our clients range from seasoned professionals to beginning students. As musicians, educators, parents of musicians and active artistic community members; we are deeply committed to the advancement of live music for all levels and representations.

Sales

Violin, Viola, Cello, Double Bass

We have a network of fine instruments and bows from makers all over the world. Private commissions are also available for your dream instrument. Don't see what you need? Please inquire as the inventory changes daily.

Rentals

Student and Professional Models

Month-to-Month with options to buy. Professional Gig Rental outfits available. Special needs? We will work something out! We provide a quality selection of rentals for the beginning students to the polished professionals.

Repairs

Instrument repair, restoration and conservation is our speciality. We perform routine maintenance, damage repair of all levels and complete restorations. We service student models and specialize in high-end, cherished instruments.
